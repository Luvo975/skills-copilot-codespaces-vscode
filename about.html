🌱
OUR FOUNDER

Miss Navoc Mdee

Set & Thrive was born from a deeply personal place. Like many people, I experienced firsthand what it feels like to pour yourself into friendships that were one-sided, to have your time disrespected, and to stay in relationships long after they had stopped being healthy.

I struggled with setting bounddaries, not because I didn't know they were needed, but because I didn't know how. Nobody had ever taught me that saying no was an act of love, or that leaving a draining friendship was not betrayal but self-preservation.

Set&Thrive exists so that nobody has to figure this out alone. It is a space where people learn to show up fully. For themselves first, and then for others.

OUR STORY

How Set & Thrive was born

Set & Thrive was created to fill a gap that most NGOs ignore, the emotional and relational side of personal well-being. While many organisations focus on big systemic issues, we focus on something equally important: the quality of the relationships we build every single day.

We believe that healthy relationships do not happen by accident. They are built intentionally, through reciprocity, honest communication, mutual effort, and respect for each other's time and energy.

What started as a personal mission has grown into a community of people who are ready to do the work, to set better boundaries, show up more intentionally, and build relationships that truly sustain them.

Our mission
What we exist to do

"To empower individuals to set intentional boundaries, manage their time with purpose, and build relationships rooted in reciprocity, honesty, and mutual growth."

Our vision
The world we are building

A world where every person knows their worth, protects their peace, and builds relationships that are equal, honest, and deeply fulfilling — because they were taught how to show up with intention.
