FAQ

Frequently asked questions

How do I attend a workshop?

Fill in the contact form above or email us at hello@setandthrive.org and we will send you details of upcoming workshops in your area.

Are your programs free?

Many of our programs and resources are completely free. Some workshops have a small fee to cover costs. We also offer sponsored spots for those who cannot afford to pay.

Can I volunteer with Set & Thrive?

Absolutely! We welcome volunteers of all backgrounds. Fill in the contact form and select "Volunteering with us" and we will be in touch with next steps.

Do you work with schools and companies?

Yes! We offer tailored workshops for schools, universities, and corporate teams. Contact us to discuss a program that fits your organisation.

How can I share my story?

We would love to hear your story. Fill in the form and select "Sharing my story" — your experience could help someone else take their first step.

How quickly will you respond?

We aim to respond to all messages within 24 hours on weekdays. For urgent matters please call or WhatsApp us directly.
