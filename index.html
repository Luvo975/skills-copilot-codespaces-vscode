THE PROBLEM

People are connected but not truly present.

We live in a world where people overcommit, underdeliver, and lose meaningful relationships
along the way. Set & Thrive exists to change that.

Lack of boundaries quietly destroy friendships and self-worth
Wasted time signals a lack of respect for others
Emotional unavailability creates distance in relationships
One sided friendships drain your energy and peace

WHAT WE STAND FOR

Three pillars, one purpose.

⏰
Time & respect
Honouring your time, and others' is the first act of real respect.
🤝
Emotional intelligence
Active listening, emotional safety, and genuine presence in every relationship.
🌱
Relational integrity
Accountability, honesty, and mutual effort are the roots of a lasting connection.

OUR MISSION

"To empower individuals to set intentional boundaries, manage their time with purpose, and build relationships rooted in reciprocity, honesty, and mutual growth."

WHAT WE DO

Our Programmes

Boundary Workshops:
Know your limits. Communicate them. We host practical sessions on setting and communicating healthy boundaries in friendships, family and the workplace. Teaching people how to gracefully navigate and exit emotionally draining friendships.
Time Circles:
Your time reflects your values. Community conversations about respecting your time and showing up consistently and intentionally for others.
Relationship Audits:
Not every relationship deserves the same energy. Guided self-reflection tools to evaluate the health, balance, and reciprocity of your most important relationships.
